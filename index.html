Welcome

I am a PhD Candidate at the TUDelft, my research is focused in the use of 3D City Models for Energy Applications. This involves simulations such as the solar potential of building's surfaces or heating and cooling demand of buildings in multiple scale of analyses (from the granularity of buildings to a national level). I also evaluate current data models to store this data and the use of artificial intelligence techniques to cope with data gaps.
